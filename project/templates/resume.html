About

Community

As a member of the Texas Developer community, I take great pride in participating in professional events. I consistently attend conferences around the country and continually learn as much as I can to stay on the forefront of technology as it evolves. I have spoken at events such as the ESRI International User Conference and the TxDOT Planning Conference presenting applications and projects which I have coordinated and developed.
Now as an employee of TNRIS, I play a significant role in the planning, facilitation, and execution of the annual TNRIS GeoRodeo Geospatial Developer Conference. This is a sponsored event hosted for developers in Texas and beyond. With hundreds of attendees each year, the GeoRodeo serves as an exploration of cutting edge, open source programming tools and projects purely for the greater good of our developer community.
